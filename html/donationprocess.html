Our Donation Process
Whether you're an NGO or a donor, all you need to do is follow a few simple steps to make a difference.
Step 1
Start A Campaign
Choose your cause and enter the required details to start your campaign.
Step 2
Campaign Is Live
Once KYC is approved, the campaign page goes live, allowing donors to donate towards your cause.
Step 3
Campaign Verification
After KYC is completed, our dedicated team will review and verify all the details provided to approve your campaign.
Step 4
Withdraw Funds
Once you start receiving donations, you can submit a withdrawal request along with any relevant bills or receipts to withdraw the funds.
